Portfolio

At Verdenova, we are proud to deliver high-impact sustainability consultancy across a wide range of sectors. Our portfolio highlights diverse projects that embody innovation, responsibility, and forward-thinking environmental stewardship. Each case reflects our ability to adapt to unique challenges and deliver sustainable solutions that create long-term value.

Showcasing Our Global Commitment to Sustainability

Commercial Buildings

Project: EcoTower Office Complex Scope: LEED Gold Certification Highlights:

  • Implementation of energy-efficient HVAC systems
  • Rainwater harvesting infrastructure
  • Green roofing and daylighting optimization Outcome: Achieved 30% energy savings and improved occupant wellness through sustainable design.

Healthcare Facilities

Project: GreenCare Medical Center Scope: BREEAM Excellent Certification Highlights:

  • Low-impact construction materials and finishes
  • Centralized waste segregation system
  • Enhanced indoor air quality protocols Outcome: Reduced hospital carbon footprint while improving healing environments for patients and staff.

Educational Institutions

Project: Sustainable Learning Academy Scope: WELL Building Standard Certification Highlights:

  • Biophilic design and ample natural lighting
  • Low-VOC materials and ergonomic classroom layouts
  • Renewable energy integration via solar arrays Outcome: Boosted student productivity and lowered operational costs through smart, green architecture.

Residential Developments

Project: Harmony Eco-Homes Community Scope: Net-Zero Energy Development Highlights:

  • Rooftop solar systems on all housing units
  • Energy-efficient appliances and smart metering
  • Neighborhood recycling and composting hubs Outcome: Delivered sustainable housing at scale, with zero net annual energy consumption.

Industrial Facilities

Project: GreenManufacture Facility Scope: ISO 14001 Environmental Management System Implementation Highlights:

  • Advanced emission control technologies
  • Water recycling systems for industrial processes
  • Workforce sustainability awareness training Outcome: Achieved regulatory compliance, waste reduction, and operational efficiency.

Government and Public Sector

Project: Smart Sustainable City Hall Scope: Energy Efficiency Retrofit Highlights:

  • LED lighting retrofits and motion sensors
  • Building automation and energy dashboards
  • Public engagement campaigns for sustainability Outcome: Enhanced transparency, reduced utility costs, and set an example for local green leadership.

Eco-Tourism & Hospitality

Project: VerdeEco Resort Scope: Sustainable Tourism Accreditation Highlights:

  • Off-grid renewable energy system
  • Water-efficient landscaping and greywater reuse
  • Locally sourced building materials and eco-friendly amenities Outcome: Created an eco-conscious guest experience with minimal ecological impact.

Corporate ESG Integration

Project: ESG Framework Rollout for a Multinational Tech Firm Scope: Full ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) Implementation Highlights:

  • Stakeholder engagement workshops
  • Materiality assessments and impact reporting
  • Alignment with GRI and SASB standards Outcome: Strengthened investor confidence and improved brand equity through credible ESG performance.

Research & Innovation Projects

Project: ESG Circular Economy Materials Lab Scope: Pilot program for zero-waste manufacturing Highlights:

  • Reuse of industrial byproducts
  • Closed-loop product lifecycle testing
  • Collaboration with academic institutions Outcome: Pioneered scalable circular economy models for sustainable production.
